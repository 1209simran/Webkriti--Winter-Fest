FASHION SHOW


Rules:-


    • 1. Each team can showcase a maximum of three rounds.
      2. Each team will be allotted 8:00-10:00 minutes for each round(stage-in to stage-out).
      3. The teams are responsible for their own music and sequence.
      4. Teams can ask for assistance from people outside the college such as choreographers, make-up artists, but these professionals will not be considered as a part of the team. These personnels will be the sole responsibility of the team.
      5. Teams are suggested to inform the organizers of any kind of props they are planning to use. Use of water, fire, pets or any hazardous materials is not allowed. Teams are instructed to consult the organizers before using any such material.
      6. Prize money worth Rs 75,000/-
