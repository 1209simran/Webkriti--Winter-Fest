CONCERT


Rules:-


    • 1. Prizes worth Rs.1 Lakh+ including rewards for best guitarist, vocalist, drummer and bassist/pianist.
      2. A total duration of 15 mins will be given to perform 4 songs for prelims and 30 mins for 5 songs during finals with 5 mins before and after each performance to decide arrangements and sound check.
      3. For Finals, an Original composition is required amongst the 5 songs.
      4. No vulgarity, profanity or sexual content should be in the lyrics, all songs should be family friendly.
      5. The song selection is open genre and will be judged based on various factors like skill level, confidence, stage presence, audience interaction etc.
      6. All decisions of the Programme Committee and the jury concerning the competition are irrevocable.
